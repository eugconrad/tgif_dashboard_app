Talkgroup Description

TG 51550 is a talkgroup for the Saipan Amateur Radio Club WH0ACI - based out of the Northern Marianas Islands Commonwealth of the United Staes, in the western Pacific Ocean.

51550 is linked to the M17 Reflector M17-SAR V and AllStarLink Node 557370.
