Talkgroup Description

72 Chew - Keeping the 40M airwaves warm!


Daily from 9am-12pm ET with a different NCS for each hour, daily on 7.272mhz (and during those hours is when this TG would be most active)...

Stop by, say hello, all are welcome to join in. The 72Chew are always looking forward to new checkins as well as the daily regulars!

Visit the webpage for more about the 72 Chew (NCS skeds, Roster List, Monthly Newsletter, upcoming Special Events and Announcements)...

Check out the latest edition of "The Chew" monthly newsletter - click on the 72 logo below to read.

There are many HF'ers that also use digital voice and to those that are familiar with the 72 Rag Chew from 40-meters will recognize this talk group AND to those not familiar with the 72 Rag Chew, stop by  7.272mhz if you have HF capability...
 
*** Please note, there is no bridge or link coming from HF... This is just a Talk Group to use when conditions on 40m are not cooperating. If you like to listen to the 72 Rag Chew, you can use one of the many SDR websites to tune-in & listen...
